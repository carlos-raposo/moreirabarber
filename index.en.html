About

Fábio's passion for the barber trade has been practically forever. This taste together with the admiration he always had for his grandfather, António Moreira, culminated in the creation of a unique salon, marked by the relaxed atmosphere and the concern to please the client as much as possible.

Fábio Moreira is a graduate from IEDCB: Institute for the Study and Development of Beauty Care in Lisbon.
